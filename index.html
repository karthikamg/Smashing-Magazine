The Current State Of E-Commerce Filtering

  • By Christian Holst
  • April 20th, 2015
  • E-Commerce Navigation Usability
  • 3 Comments

When done right, filters enable users to narrow down a website selection of thousands of products to only those few items that match their particular needs and interests. Yet, despite it being a central aspect of the user e-commerce product browsing, most websites offer a lacklustre filtering experience. In fact, our 2015 benchmark reveals that only 16% of major e-commerce websites offer a reasonably good filtering experience.
